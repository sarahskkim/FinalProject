About Me






I was born in Queens, NY and raised in a small town in NJ, named Manalapan.
I was born with a physical disability, cerebral palsy. Some aspects of my life
was an uphill battle, such as fighting for the education I well-deserved. But I
continue to conquer the battles one at a time, and I am really blessed that I
made it to Barnard. I know have the mindset of that I can really achieve
anything I put my heart into, and I'm truly, as cliche as it is, majoring in
unafraid at Barnard.
